# Condition

Some Vega 2.4 vehicles may exhibit a pulsation or judder felt through the brake pedal and steering wheel during moderate-to-firm braking from highway speeds. The condition is caused by lateral runout and thickness variation in the front brake rotors, part number BR-7731. Customers typically notice the judder intensifying as the rotors heat up during repeated stops. See the [Vega Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Sub-System](doc://man-vega-brakes#front-brake-sub-system) for the front corner layout.

# Cause

The judder originates from disc thickness variation developing in front rotors BR-7731, accelerated by uneven pad transfer film and high-heat duty cycles. Once measurable runout exceeds the service limit, machining is no longer recommended and rotor replacement is required. The [Vega Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Pad and Rotor Service](doc://man-vega-brakes#front-brake-sub-system/front-brake-pad-and-rotor-service) lists runout and minimum thickness specifications, and the [Kestrel Brakes Service Manual :: Brake System Overview](doc://man-kestrel-brakes#brake-system-overview) describes comparable rotor wear patterns on the related platform.

# Repair Procedure

Replace both front rotors, part number BR-7731, and the front brake pads, part number BP-7720, as a matched set following the [Vega Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Pad and Rotor Service](doc://man-vega-brakes#front-brake-sub-system/front-brake-pad-and-rotor-service) (PROC-BRAKE-FR). Do not machine the new rotors; install them dry and clean per the [Vega Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Pad Replacement](doc://man-vega-brakes#front-brake-sub-system/front-brake-pad-replacement) and bed the pads with a controlled burnishing sequence. After service, road test to confirm the judder is eliminated and inspect for even pad contact. Refer to the analogous [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Pad and Rotor Service Procedure](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#front-brakes/front-brake-pad-and-rotor-service-procedure) for bedding technique on platforms sharing this caliper family.
