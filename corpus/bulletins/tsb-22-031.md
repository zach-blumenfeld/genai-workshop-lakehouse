title: Brake Judder After Front Pad Replacement
area: bulletins
---

# Condition

Falcon and Osprey vehicles may exhibit steering-wheel judder under braking
shortly after a front brake pad change. Affected parts are the BP-7720 pad kit
and BR-7731 rotor pair.

# Cause

Reusing rotors beyond runout spec, or machining the BR-7731 (not approved),
leaves thickness variation that produces judder.

# Repair Procedure

Replace the BR-7731 rotor pair together with the BP-7720 pads; do not machine.
Follow [Front Brake Service](doc://man-fal-3#brakes/front-brake-service) and
torque to spec. Inspect the BH-7745 brake hose while the caliper is off.
