# Condition

Owners of Vega 2.4 vehicles may report a low-frequency groan or squeal during light-to-moderate braking, often accompanied by a pulsation or judder felt through the brake pedal and steering wheel. The condition is most noticeable at highway speeds during gradual deceleration and tends to worsen after the vehicle has been parked overnight in humid conditions. For an overview of the front brake assembly and how the symptom maps to the system, see [Vega Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Sub-System](doc://man-vega-brakes#front-brake-sub-system). A comparable noise-and-judder presentation is described in [Kestrel Brakes Service Manual :: Brake System Overview](doc://man-kestrel-brakes#brake-system-overview).

# Cause

The condition is caused by uneven friction-material transfer from front brake pad set BP-7720 onto the surface of front brake rotor BR-7731, producing localized thickness variation that drives the judder. Early production BP-7720 pads used a friction formulation prone to deposit buildup, which also accounts for the groan noise as the deposits load and unload during braking. Refer to [Vega Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Pad and Rotor Service](doc://man-vega-brakes#front-brake-sub-system/front-brake-pad-and-rotor-service) for inspection criteria, and compare pad and rotor wear limits against [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Front Pad and Rotor Specifications](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#front-brakes/front-pad-and-rotor-specifications).

# Repair Procedure

Replace front brake pad set BP-7720 and resurface or replace front brake rotor BR-7731 as a set following procedure PROC-BRAKE-FR, then perform a proper pad bed-in to establish an even transfer layer. Detailed torque values and caliper handling steps are provided in [Vega Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Pad Replacement](doc://man-vega-brakes#front-brake-sub-system/front-brake-pad-replacement). If rotor thickness on BR-7731 is below the discard specification, replace rather than machine; the equivalent procedure for reference is [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Pad and Rotor Service Procedure](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#front-brakes/front-brake-pad-and-rotor-service-procedure). Road test to confirm the judder and noise are resolved before release.
