# Condition

Owners of the Harrier 3.0 TD may report coolant loss, an over-temperature warning, or an illuminated MIL with DTC P0217 (Engine Over-Temperature Condition) stored. In some cases DTC P0128 (Coolant Thermostat Below Regulating Temperature) is also present if the thermostat has been disturbed during prior service. A pink coolant residue trail behind the front cover indicates seepage from the water pump weep hole. Review the code-setting logic in [Harrier Cooling Service Manual :: Thermostat and Temperature Faults](doc://man-harrier-cooling#cooling-system-diagnosis/thermostat-and-temperature-faults) before condemning any part.

# Cause

The P0217 over-temperature condition is caused by a failing internal seal in water pump WP-6122, which allows coolant to weep and progressively reduces flow through the engine. As coolant level drops, circulation degrades and engine temperature climbs beyond the protection threshold. If thermostat TSTAT-6100 was previously replaced with an incorrect opening temperature, it can compound the condition by delaying coolant flow. Confirm the leak source against [Harrier Cooling Service Manual :: Coolant Circulation](doc://man-harrier-cooling#coolant-circulation) and verify WP-6122 part eligibility using [Harrier Cooling Service Manual :: Water Pump Identification and Supersession](doc://man-harrier-cooling#coolant-circulation/water-pump-identification-and-supersession).

# Repair Procedure

Replace water pump WP-6122 following PROC-WATER-PUMP as detailed in the [Harrier Cooling Service Manual :: Water Pump Replacement Procedure](doc://man-harrier-cooling#coolant-circulation/water-pump-replacement-procedure). Inspect thermostat TSTAT-6100 and replace it if the opening temperature is out of specification, referring to the [Harrier Cooling Service Manual :: Thermostat Replacement Procedure](doc://man-harrier-cooling#temperature-regulation/thermostat-replacement-procedure). Refill and bleed the system per [Harrier Cooling Service Manual :: Temperature Regulation](doc://man-harrier-cooling#temperature-regulation), then clear P0217 and verify normal operating temperature on a road test. Additional component locations are documented in the [Harrier Cooling Service Manual](doc://man-harrier-cooling).
