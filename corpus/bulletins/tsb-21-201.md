# Condition

The Heron 2.5 may set DTC P0351 (Ignition Coil "A" Primary/Secondary Circuit Malfunction) accompanied by a rough idle, hesitation under load, and an illuminated MIL. Drivers often describe a stumble that worsens when the engine is cold or under acceleration. Confirm P0351 with a scan tool and verify the misfire correlates to the cylinder served by the cylinder-1 coil. Begin diagnosis using the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Circuit Diagnosis](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-circuit-diagnosis) to isolate primary versus secondary faults.

# Cause

P0351 on this application is most commonly caused by internal breakdown of the cylinder-1 ignition coil, part number IC-2044-A, where the secondary winding insulation degrades under heat and vibration. As the coil weakens, spark energy collapses intermittently and the ECM records the primary/secondary circuit fault. Confirm the failure by swapping IC-2044-A to an adjacent cylinder and observing whether the misfire follows the coil. The [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Coil Primary and Secondary Circuit Faults](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-circuit-diagnosis/coil-primary-and-secondary-circuit-faults) describes the resistance and waveform checks, and [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Application-Specific Coils](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils/application-specific-coils) confirms IC-2044-A applicability.

# Repair Procedure

Replace the failed coil IC-2044-A following PROC-IGN-COIL, and inspect the corresponding spark plug SP-1108 for fouling or excessive gap. If SP-1108 shows wear, replace it as part of the repair to prevent a repeat P0351 from a high-resistance secondary path. Torque the coil hold-down and reconnect the primary connector fully until it clicks. Step-by-step removal and installation are in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Coil Replacement Procedure](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils/ignition-coil-replacement-procedure), and plug gap and heat-range data are in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Spark Plug Selection and Specifications](doc://man-heron-ignition#spark-plugs/spark-plug-selection-and-specifications). For overall system context refer to the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Coils](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils) section.
