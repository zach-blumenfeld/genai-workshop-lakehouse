# Condition

The customer may report intermittent or complete loss of power steering assist on the Lumen 2.0T, accompanied by an EPS warning lamp and DTC C1556 (EPS Torque Sensor Fault) stored in the steering control module. Steering effort increases significantly when assist is lost, most noticeably at low speeds and during parking maneuvers. The condition may be temperature dependent and can self-recover after a key cycle. Confirm the stored code and freeze-frame data per [Lumen Steering Service Manual :: EPS Torque Sensor Fault](doc://man-lumen-steering#diagnostic-trouble-codes/eps-torque-sensor-fault).

# Cause

C1556 is caused by an internal torque sensor fault within the electric power steering assembly EPS-8740, where the sensor signal falls outside the valid range and the module disables assist as a safety measure. Connector corrosion or a damaged sensor pigtail at EPS-8740 can produce the same symptom and should be ruled out first. Review the operating principle and signal expectations in [Lumen Steering Service Manual :: Electric Power Steering Motor](doc://man-lumen-steering#steering-system-overview/electric-power-steering-motor) and the broader code list in [Lumen Steering Service Manual :: Diagnostic Trouble Codes](doc://man-lumen-steering#diagnostic-trouble-codes).

# Repair Procedure

Diagnose the EPS-8740 assembly following PROC-EPS-DIAG as documented in the [Lumen Steering Service Manual :: Electric Power Steering Diagnosis Procedure](doc://man-lumen-steering#electric-power-steering-sub-system-service/electric-power-steering-diagnosis-procedure). If the torque sensor signal is confirmed out of range and wiring is intact, replace EPS-8740 per the [Lumen Steering Service Manual :: Electric Power Steering Sub-System Service](doc://man-lumen-steering#electric-power-steering-sub-system-service). After replacement, perform the required torque sensor calibration, clear C1556, and verify full assist returns through the steering range. System layout and component identification are covered in the [Lumen Steering Service Manual :: Steering System Overview](doc://man-lumen-steering#steering-system-overview).
