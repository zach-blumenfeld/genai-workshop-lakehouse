---
id: tsb-27-110
title: "Osprey 3.5 V6: Torque converter clutch stuck off due to shift solenoid pack"
area: bulletins
---

# Condition

Affected Osprey 3.5 V6 vehicles may set DTC P0741 (Torque Converter Clutch Circuit Performance / Stuck Off), often accompanied by the general transmission code P0700. Drivers may report elevated RPM at highway cruise, reduced fuel economy, and a transmission overheat warning during sustained operation. Review the [Diagnostic Trouble Code Reference](doc://man-osprey-transmission#diagnosis/diagnostic-trouble-code-reference) and the [Torque Converter Clutch Diagnosis](doc://man-osprey-transmission#shift-control-sub-system/torque-converter-clutch-diagnosis) before condemning any component.

# Cause

The condition is caused by a stuck TCC apply valve within shift solenoid pack TSOL-9520, which prevents the torque converter clutch from engaging and sets P0741. Internal valve contamination or wear holds the apply circuit open regardless of commanded duty cycle. Confirm the hydraulic root cause using the [Fluid Pressure Diagnosis](doc://man-osprey-transmission#diagnosis/fluid-pressure-diagnosis) and the [Transmission Fault Diagnosis Procedure](doc://man-osprey-transmission#diagnosis/transmission-fault-diagnosis-procedure) rather than replacing the converter outright.

# Repair Procedure

After confirming the diagnosis, replace shift solenoid pack TSOL-9520 following PROC-SHIFT-SOL and the [Shift Solenoid Pack Replacement Procedure](doc://man-osprey-transmission#shift-control-sub-system/shift-solenoid-pack-replacement-procedure). General orientation for the affected assembly is provided in the [Transmission System Overview](doc://man-osprey-transmission#transmission-system-overview) and the [Shift Control Sub-System](doc://man-osprey-transmission#shift-control-sub-system) section. After repair, clear P0741 and P0700, then verify torque converter clutch apply during a road test.
