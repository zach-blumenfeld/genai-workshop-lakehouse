title: Heron HVAC blower motor circuit failure causing inoperative cabin fan

# Condition

Affected Heron vehicles may store DTC B1352 (Blower Motor Circuit Failure) with a completely inoperative cabin fan CABF-7280 at all speed settings. The customer typically reports no airflow from the vents regardless of temperature or mode selection. Confirm the fault is circuit-related rather than a blown fuse or control-head issue, and review the [Blower Motor and Cabin Air Filter](doc://man-heron-hvac#hvac-system-overview/blower-motor-and-cabin-air-filter) overview before disassembly. The [Diagnostic Trouble Codes](doc://man-heron-hvac#diagnostic-trouble-codes) section lists the B1352 set conditions.

# Cause

The condition is caused by an open winding in the blower motor BLW-7200, which interrupts current flow and triggers the B1352 fault while leaving cabin fan CABF-7280 inoperative. Thermal cycling and brush wear accelerate the internal failure over time. Refer to the [Blower Motor Circuit Code](doc://man-heron-hvac#diagnostic-trouble-codes/blower-motor-circuit-code) for the precise circuit description, and review the [HVAC System Overview](doc://man-heron-hvac#hvac-system-overview) to understand how BLW-7200 drives airflow through the system.

# Repair Procedure

Perform PROC-BLOWER to confirm the open circuit in blower motor BLW-7200 and replace the unit following the [HVAC Blower Motor Replacement Procedure](doc://man-heron-hvac#air-distribution-sub-system-service/hvac-blower-motor-replacement-procedure) in the [Air Distribution Sub-System Service](doc://man-heron-hvac#air-distribution-sub-system-service) section. While the assembly is accessed, inspect and if necessary service the filter using the [Cabin Air Filter Replacement Procedure](doc://man-heron-hvac#air-distribution-sub-system-service/cabin-air-filter-replacement-procedure). After replacing BLW-7200, clear B1352 and verify cabin fan CABF-7280 operates at all speeds. For comparison on similar HVAC complaints, the [Marlin EV Hvac Service Manual :: HVAC System Diagnosis](doc://man-marlin-ev-hvac#hvac-system-diagnosis) may provide supplemental guidance.
