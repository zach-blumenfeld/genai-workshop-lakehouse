# Condition

Heron 2.5 owners may report a harsh or jarring ride over rough pavement, accompanied by a clunk or knock from the front of the vehicle on bumps and during cornering. The symptom is often worse on one side and may include excessive nose dive under braking or reduced directional stability. For the front suspension layout and component identification, see [Heron Suspension Service Manual :: Front Strut Sub-System](doc://man-heron-suspension#front-strut-sub-system). A comparable harshness presentation is documented in [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Front Strut Assembly](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#suspension-system-overview/front-strut-assembly).

# Cause

The condition is caused by internal wear of front strut assembly STRT-8500, which loses damping capacity and allows the upper mount to knock as the unit cycles. Worn STRT-8500 units exhibit oil weepage and reduced rebound control, producing both the harsh ride and the audible clunk. General strut wear background is covered in [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Suspension System Overview](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#suspension-system-overview).

# Repair Procedure

Replace front strut assembly STRT-8500 in axle pairs following procedure PROC-STRUT to maintain balanced damping side to side. Detailed spring compression and torque steps are provided in [Heron Suspension Service Manual :: Front Strut Replacement](doc://man-heron-suspension#front-strut-sub-system/front-strut-replacement); an equivalent reference procedure is [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Front Strut Replacement Procedure](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#front-strut-sub-system-service/front-strut-replacement-procedure).

# Post-Repair Alignment

After replacing STRT-8500, a four-wheel alignment is required because strut service disturbs front camber and caster settings. Perform alignment per procedure PROC-ALIGN and verify all angles fall within factory specification before release. The full alignment sequence is documented in [Lumen Steering Service Manual :: Four-Wheel Alignment Procedure](doc://man-lumen-steering#wheel-alignment-sub-system-service/four-wheel-alignment-procedure).
