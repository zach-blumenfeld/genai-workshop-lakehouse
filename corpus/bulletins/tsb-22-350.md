# Condition

Operators of the Ridgeline HD 6.7 TD may report a metallic clunk over bumps and an uneven or bouncy ride, particularly when loaded or towing. The noise is most evident on broken pavement and during low-speed turning maneuvers. Inspect the front strut assembly STRT-8500 for fluid leakage, a damaged mount, and excessive rod play. Confirm the symptom against the description in the [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Front Strut Assembly](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#suspension-system-overview/front-strut-assembly) before condemning components.

# Cause

The clunk and ride degradation are caused by internal wear of the front strut STRT-8500, including a worn upper mount bearing and loss of damping force. High-mileage units operated under heavy GVWR loads experience accelerated seal and valving wear. This bulletin supersedes and complements prior diagnoses; if a worn lower control arm is also suspected, see [Ridgeline HD: Front clunk and uneven tire wear from worn lower control arm](doc://tsb-20-508), and for related knock symptoms refer to [Ridgeline HD front strut knock from worn strut assembly](doc://tsb-21-211). General system context is provided in the [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Suspension System Overview](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#suspension-system-overview).

# Repair Procedure

Replace the affected front strut assembly STRT-8500 in axle pairs following [PROC-STRUT] as documented in the [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Front Strut Replacement Procedure](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#front-strut-sub-system-service/front-strut-replacement-procedure). Support the lower control arm during disassembly and torque the strut-to-knuckle and upper mount fasteners to the values in the [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Front Strut Sub-System Service](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#front-strut-sub-system-service) chapter. After installation, perform a four-wheel alignment and road test to confirm the clunk and ride quality are corrected; consult the full [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension) for fastener specifications.
