# Condition

Owners of affected Marlin EV vehicles may report front brake pad BP-7720 wearing out well before the expected service interval, often accompanied by visible scoring or lipping of front rotor BR-7731. A brake-wear warning or grinding noise during light braking is a common first symptom. The condition is most pronounced in vehicles operated primarily in stop-and-go driving where regenerative braking is less effective. See [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Front Pad and Rotor Specifications](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#front-brakes/front-pad-and-rotor-specifications) for the wear limits used to confirm the condition.

# Cause

The premature wear of pad BP-7720 and rotor BR-7731 is caused by a calibration that under-applies regenerative braking on early units, shifting more of the braking duty to the friction system. The resulting higher mechanical load accelerates pad and rotor wear far beyond normal expectations. Background on the front friction system is provided in [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brakes](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#front-brakes). A general overview of the brake system is also available in [Kestrel Brakes Service Manual :: Brake System Overview](doc://man-kestrel-brakes#brake-system-overview).

# Repair Procedure

Replace front brake pads BP-7720 and rotors BR-7731 as a set per PROC-BRAKE-FR, then apply the updated regenerative braking calibration to prevent recurrence. Bed in the new friction components according to the manual before returning the vehicle. Complete service steps are detailed in [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Pad and Rotor Service Procedure](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#front-brakes/front-brake-pad-and-rotor-service-procedure). For comparable front-brake service references, see [Vega Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Pad and Rotor Service](doc://man-vega-brakes#front-brake-sub-system/front-brake-pad-and-rotor-service) and [Vega Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Pad Replacement](doc://man-vega-brakes#front-brake-sub-system/front-brake-pad-replacement).
