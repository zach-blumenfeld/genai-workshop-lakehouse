# Condition: Rough Idle on Heron 2.5

Affected Heron 2.5 vehicles may exhibit a rough idle, intermittent misfire feel, and an illuminated check engine lamp. The PCM stores DTC P0352, indicating an ignition coil "B" primary control circuit malfunction. The condition is most pronounced during cold-start idle and tends to settle once the engine reaches operating temperature. Drivers commonly report a faint exhaust pulsation accompanying the rough idle.

# Cause: Coil B Primary Circuit Fault

The root cause is degradation of the cylinder 2 ignition coil, part number IC-2044-A, where intermittent opens in the primary winding trigger P0352. Heat-cycling fatigue at the coil connector pin is the dominant failure mode, producing a high-resistance primary circuit that the PCM detects as a control-circuit fault. For background on the coil family and its driver-stage monitoring, see the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Coils](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils) and confirm the correct application using [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Application-Specific Coils](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils/application-specific-coils). Detailed primary-circuit fault isolation is covered under [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Coil Primary and Secondary Circuit Faults](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-circuit-diagnosis/coil-primary-and-secondary-circuit-faults).

# Repair Procedure: Ignition Coil and Spark Plug Service

Replace the cylinder 2 coil IC-2044-A and, while access is open, renew the matching spark plug SP-1110 to restore consistent secondary output. Follow PROC-IGN-COIL for coil removal and torque, referencing the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Coil Replacement Procedure](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils/ignition-coil-replacement-procedure), then complete PROC-SPARK-PLUG per the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Spark Plug Replacement Procedure](doc://man-heron-ignition#spark-plugs/spark-plug-replacement-procedure). Verify the SP-1110 gap and heat range against [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Spark Plug Selection and Specifications](doc://man-heron-ignition#spark-plugs/spark-plug-selection-and-specifications) before reassembly. After repair, clear P0352 and run an idle relearn following the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Circuit Diagnosis](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-circuit-diagnosis) verification steps to confirm the rough idle is resolved.
