id: tsb-26-110
title: Vega lost communication with body control module from chafed harness
area: bulletins
---

# Condition

Affected Vega vehicles may store DTC U0140 (Lost Communication With Body Control Module) and the instrument cluster may go dark with multiple inoperative body electrical features. The body control module BCM-9920 will intermittently drop off the CAN bus, often when the vehicle traverses rough pavement. Technicians should confirm the fault is communication-related before condemning BCM-9920, and review the [Vega Electrical Service Manual :: Body Control Module](doc://man-vega-electrical#electrical-system-overview/body-control-module) for normal network behavior. See also the [Network Communication Diagnostic Codes](doc://man-vega-electrical#network-communication-diagnostic-codes) section for the full U0140 description.

# Cause

The root cause is the main body harness HARN-9960 chafing against an unprotected bracket edge behind the left kick panel, which abrades the CAN circuit conductors and produces the intermittent U0140 fault. Once the insulation wears through, the bus shorts intermittently and BCM-9920 loses arbitration. Refer to the [Wiring Harness and Fuse Box](doc://man-vega-electrical#electrical-system-overview/wiring-harness-and-fuse-box) overview to locate the affected harness routing, and to [Lost Communication with BCM and Cluster](doc://man-vega-electrical#network-communication-diagnostic-codes/lost-communication-with-bcm-and-cluster) for symptom correlation.

# Repair Procedure

Begin diagnosis with PROC-COMMS-DIAG to verify the CAN bus fault and isolate the chafed segment of harness HARN-9960, following the [CAN Bus Communication Diagnosis Procedure](doc://man-vega-electrical#network-and-harness-sub-system-service/can-bus-communication-diagnosis-procedure). Repair or replace the damaged HARN-9960 conductors, add protective convolute, and reroute the harness clear of the bracket edge per the [Network and Harness Sub-System Service](doc://man-vega-electrical#network-and-harness-sub-system-service) instructions. After harness repair, run PROC-BCM-CONFIG to reload BCM-9920 calibrations as described in the [Body Control Module Configuration Procedure](doc://man-vega-electrical#bcm-sub-system-service/body-control-module-configuration-procedure). Clear U0140, confirm the cluster powers up normally, and consult the [Wiring Diagrams and Reference](doc://man-vega-electrical#wiring-diagrams-and-reference) if pin-level verification is required.
