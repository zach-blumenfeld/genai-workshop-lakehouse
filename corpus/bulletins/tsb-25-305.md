# Condition

Some Marlin EV vehicles may exhibit uneven braking, a slight pull, or a soft pedal that recovers after several seconds. The symptom traces to an internal restriction in an original-production front brake hose, which limits hydraulic flow and pressure release at one corner. The corrected service part is brake hose BH-7745. General orientation on the affected circuit is available in the [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes).

# Cause

Affected front brake hoses can develop an internal liner flap that intermittently obstructs fluid flow under heavy braking. Because the restriction is internal, the hose passes a visual inspection and the condition is confirmed only by the uneven braking symptom. Replacement hose BH-7745 incorporates a revised liner that eliminates the flap. The hydraulic routing for this circuit is detailed in the [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Hydraulic Lines and Fluid](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#hydraulic-lines-and-fluid).

# Repair Procedure

Replace the affected front brake hose with BH-7745 following procedure PROC-BRAKE-HOSE as described in the [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Brake Hose Replacement Procedure](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#hydraulic-lines-and-fluid/brake-hose-replacement-procedure). After the hose is installed, perform a full flush and bleed with fluid BF-7790 per procedure PROC-BRAKE-FLUID, referencing the [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Brake Fluid Flush and Bleed Procedure](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#hydraulic-lines-and-fluid/brake-fluid-flush-and-bleed-procedure). Confirm a firm pedal and even braking on a road test before returning the vehicle. Torque all fittings to specification listed in the [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes).
