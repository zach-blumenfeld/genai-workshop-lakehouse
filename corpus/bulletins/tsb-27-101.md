title: "Heron 2.5: Ignition coil B primary/secondary circuit fault causing cylinder 2 misfire"

---

# Condition

Heron 2.5 vehicles may set diagnostic trouble code P0352 (ignition coil B primary/secondary circuit) accompanied by P0302 (cylinder 2 misfire detected). The driver may report a rough idle, hesitation under load, and an illuminated or flashing malfunction indicator lamp. In most cases P0352 is the causal code and P0302 is the resulting combustion fault on the number two cylinder. For symptom-based misfire triage consult the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Circuit Diagnosis](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-circuit-diagnosis).

# Cause

The condition is caused by an internal winding breakdown in the application-specific ignition coil IC-2044-A serving cylinder 2, which interrupts the primary/secondary circuit and triggers P0352. Heat cycling degrades the coil's secondary insulation, producing intermittent spark loss before progressing to a hard fault. Confirm the fault is coil-related and not a harness or driver issue using the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Coil Primary and Secondary Circuit Faults](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-circuit-diagnosis/coil-primary-and-secondary-circuit-faults). Coil application details are covered in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Application-Specific Coils](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils/application-specific-coils).

# Repair Procedure

Follow PROC-IGN-COIL to remove the failed coil and install a new IC-2044-A on cylinder 2, torquing the hold-down fastener to specification. Inspect the spark plug SP-1110 at the affected cylinder; if fouled or eroded beyond limit, replace it before reassembly. Step-by-step removal and installation is documented in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Coil Replacement Procedure](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils/ignition-coil-replacement-procedure), and gap and heat-range data for SP-1110 are in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Spark Plug Selection and Specifications](doc://man-heron-ignition#spark-plugs/spark-plug-selection-and-specifications). After repair, clear P0352 and P0302 and verify no misfire counts accumulate during a road test; for broader misfire correlation refer to the [Falcon Engine Service Manual :: Misfire Diagnosis](doc://man-falcon-engine#misfire-diagnosis).
