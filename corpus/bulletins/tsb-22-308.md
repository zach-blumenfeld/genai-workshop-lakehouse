title: "Heron 2.5: Rough idle and P0352 caused by failed ignition coil B"
area: bulletins
---

# Condition

Certain Heron 2.5 vehicles may exhibit a rough idle, intermittent misfire, and an illuminated MIL with DTC P0352 (Ignition Coil "B" Primary/Secondary Circuit) stored in the PCM. The condition is most noticeable during cold start and light-load cruise, and may be accompanied by a perceptible loss of power. Confirm the complaint by retrieving stored and pending codes; if P0352 is present on its own, follow this bulletin rather than performing a full circuit teardown. For background on how this code is set, refer to the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Coil Primary and Secondary Circuit Faults](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-circuit-diagnosis/coil-primary-and-secondary-circuit-faults).

# Cause

The root cause has been traced to a manufacturing defect in early-production ignition coil B, part number IC-2044-A, in which the secondary winding insulation degrades under thermal cycling. As the insulation breaks down, the coil can no longer sustain a consistent spark event on cylinder 2, setting P0352 and producing the rough idle complaint. This is an application-specific coil; do not substitute a coil from another driveline. See [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Application-Specific Coils](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils/application-specific-coils) for the correct coil-to-application matrix and the broader [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Coils](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils) overview.

# Repair Procedure

Replace ignition coil B (IC-2044-A) following PROC-IGN-COIL and inspect the corresponding spark plug SP-1110 for fouling or electrode wear while access is available. If the spark plug shows abnormal deposits, replace it per the specifications in [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Spark Plug Selection and Specifications](doc://man-heron-ignition#spark-plugs/spark-plug-selection-and-specifications). Torque and removal steps for the coil are detailed in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Coil Replacement Procedure](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils/ignition-coil-replacement-procedure). After installation, clear P0352 and verify no misfire counts accumulate; if the code returns, perform full circuit diagnosis per the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Circuit Diagnosis](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-circuit-diagnosis).
