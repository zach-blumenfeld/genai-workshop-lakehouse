---
id: tsb-23-127
title: "Kestrel 1.5T: Catalyst efficiency below threshold from degraded upstream oxygen sensor"
area: bulletins
---

# Condition

Some Kestrel 1.5T vehicles may illuminate the MIL with stored DTC P0420, catalyst system efficiency below threshold on Bank 1. The catalytic converter CAT-5500 may appear to underperform during the catalyst monitor, even though the converter itself is within specification. Customers usually report no drivability concern aside from the warning lamp. See [Kestrel Emissions Service Manual :: Catalyst Efficiency Faults](doc://man-kestrel-emissions#emissions-system-diagnosis/catalyst-efficiency-faults) for the definition of P0420.

# Cause

The root cause is a slow or degraded upstream oxygen sensor O2U-5100, whose lazy switching skews the air-fuel feedback and causes the catalyst monitor to misjudge converter efficiency. This produces a false-positive DTC P0420 without actual failure of catalytic converter CAT-5500. Confirm sensor response and compare upstream versus downstream signals per [Kestrel Emissions Service Manual :: Upstream and Downstream Oxygen Sensors](doc://man-kestrel-emissions#oxygen-sensing/upstream-and-downstream-oxygen-sensors) before condemning the converter described in [Kestrel Emissions Service Manual :: Catalytic Converter (Bank 1 and Bank 2)](doc://man-kestrel-emissions#catalytic-conversion/catalytic-converter-bank-1-and-bank-2).

# Repair Procedure

Replace the upstream oxygen sensor O2U-5100 following procedure PROC-O2-SENSOR as detailed in [Kestrel Emissions Service Manual :: Oxygen Sensor Replacement Procedure](doc://man-kestrel-emissions#oxygen-sensing/oxygen-sensor-replacement-procedure). Review [Kestrel Emissions Service Manual :: Oxygen Sensing](doc://man-kestrel-emissions#oxygen-sensing) for connector routing and torque before installation. After replacement, clear DTC P0420 and run the catalyst and oxygen sensor readiness monitors to verify the repair, following [Kestrel Emissions Service Manual :: Emissions System Diagnosis](doc://man-kestrel-emissions#emissions-system-diagnosis). Do not replace the converter unless the monitor still fails with a verified-good sensor installed.
