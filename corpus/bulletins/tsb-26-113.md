title: Osprey torque converter clutch stuck off causing shudder and TCC code
area: bulletins
---

# Condition

Affected Osprey vehicles may set DTC P0741 (Torque Converter Clutch Circuit Performance or Stuck Off) accompanied by a noticeable shudder during light acceleration and elevated highway RPM. The torque converter clutch fails to lock up because solenoid TSOL-9520 is not commanding apply pressure. Confirm the complaint with a road test and scan data before teardown, and review the [Transmission System Overview](doc://man-osprey-transmission#transmission-system-overview) for normal TCC operation. The [Diagnostic Trouble Code Reference](doc://man-osprey-transmission#diagnosis/diagnostic-trouble-code-reference) section describes the P0741 set criteria.

# Cause

The condition is caused by the torque converter clutch solenoid TSOL-9520 sticking in the off position, frequently aggravated by degraded or contaminated transmission fluid TFLT-9500 that varnishes the solenoid valve. The restricted fluid path prevents the solenoid from achieving clean apply pressure, producing the P0741 fault. Refer to the [Torque Converter Clutch Diagnosis](doc://man-osprey-transmission#shift-control-sub-system/torque-converter-clutch-diagnosis) and the [Fluid Pressure Diagnosis](doc://man-osprey-transmission#diagnosis/fluid-pressure-diagnosis) to confirm whether TSOL-9520 or fluid condition is the dominant factor.

# Repair Procedure

Begin with a [Fluid Level and Condition Inspection](doc://man-osprey-transmission#fluid-and-filter-sub-system/fluid-level-and-condition-inspection) to evaluate transmission fluid TFLT-9500 for varnish or contamination. Perform PROC-SHIFT-SOL to replace the stuck solenoid TSOL-9520 following the [Shift Solenoid Pack Replacement Procedure](doc://man-osprey-transmission#shift-control-sub-system/shift-solenoid-pack-replacement-procedure) in the [Shift Control Sub-System](doc://man-osprey-transmission#shift-control-sub-system). Complete PROC-TRANS-SERVICE to flush and refill TFLT-9500 per the [Transmission Fluid and Filter Service Procedure](doc://man-osprey-transmission#fluid-and-filter-sub-system/transmission-fluid-and-filter-service-procedure). Clear P0741, road test to confirm the TCC now locks up smoothly with no shudder.
