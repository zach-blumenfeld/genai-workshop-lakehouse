title: "Falcon 2.0T: Cylinder 1 misfire from cracked ignition coil insulation"
area: bulletins
---

# Condition

Falcon 2.0T vehicles may set diagnostic trouble code P0301 (Cylinder 1 Misfire Detected) with a flashing or steady malfunction indicator lamp and a noticeable rough idle. The misfire is typically most pronounced under load or in damp conditions. Confirm P0301 is cylinder-specific and not part of a multi-cylinder pattern before proceeding. Misfire isolation guidance is provided in the [Falcon Engine Service Manual :: Misfire Diagnosis](doc://man-falcon-engine#misfire-diagnosis) section.

# Cause

The misfire originates from cracked insulation on the original ignition coil, part number IC-2042-A, which permits spark arc-over to the cylinder head under high secondary voltage demand. The coil has been superseded by the revised IC-2042-B, which uses an improved epoxy insulation jacket to resist cracking. Coil identification and supersession history is detailed in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Coil Identification and Supersession](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils/coil-identification-and-supersession). Background on coil construction appears in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Coils](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils) chapter.

# Repair Procedure

Replace the cylinder 1 ignition coil with the superseded IC-2042-B and install a new spark plug SP-1108, following procedure PROC-IGN-COIL for connector handling and torque values. Clear P0301 and verify misfire counters return to zero on a road test. The replacement steps are documented in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Coil Replacement Procedure](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils/ignition-coil-replacement-procedure), and spark plug gap specifications appear in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Spark Plug Selection and Specifications](doc://man-heron-ignition#spark-plugs/spark-plug-selection-and-specifications).

# Parts Information

The superseding ignition coil IC-2042-B fully replaces the discontinued IC-2042-A; do not reuse or reorder the IC-2042-A part number. Each repair requires one IC-2042-B coil and one SP-1108 spark plug for the affected cylinder. Refer to the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Spark Plugs](doc://man-heron-ignition#spark-plugs) section for plug application notes and the [Falcon Engine Service Manual](doc://man-falcon-engine) for engine-specific torque references.
