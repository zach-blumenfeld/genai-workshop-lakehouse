id: rc-2023-06
title: Ridgeline HD lower control arm may fracture causing loss of control
area: recalls
---

# Defect Description

Certain Ridgeline HD trucks were built with a lower control arm assembly, part number CARM-8540, that contains a forging void at the ball-joint boss. Under repeated heavy-load articulation the void can propagate into a crack and ultimately fracture the control arm. Because the arm shares mounting geometry with the wheel hub bearing assembly WHB-8580, a fracture also unseats the bearing and produces severe wheel misalignment. See the [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Control Arms and Sway Bar Links](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#suspension-system-overview/control-arms-and-sway-bar-links) for the affected geometry and the [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Wheel Hub Bearing Assembly](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#suspension-system-overview/wheel-hub-bearing-assembly) for the coupled hub interface.

# Safety Risk and Affected Vehicles

A fracture of CARM-8540 while the vehicle is in motion can cause sudden loss of steering control and a road-departure crash without warning. The condition is most likely under towing or full-payload conditions where articulation loads peak. Affected vehicles are Ridgeline HD units within the identified production window; refer to the [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension) and the [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Suspension System Overview](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#suspension-system-overview) to confirm the suspension configuration before inspection.

# Remedy

Dealers will replace the lower control arm CARM-8540 with a re-forged part free of charge following procedure PROC-CONTROL-ARM, and will inspect the adjacent wheel hub bearing assembly WHB-8580 for collateral damage. Perform the replacement per the [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Control Arm Replacement Procedure](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#control-arm-and-sway-bar-sub-system-service/control-arm-replacement-procedure), and if the hub shows play replace it using the [Ridgeline HD Suspension Service Manual :: Wheel Hub Bearing Replacement Procedure](doc://man-ridgeline-hd-suspension#wheel-hub-bearing-sub-system-service/wheel-hub-bearing-replacement-procedure). Owners can verify recall status and remedy availability at https://www.nhtsa.gov/recalls before scheduling service.
