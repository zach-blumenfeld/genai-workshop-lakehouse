title: "Safety Recall - Ignition Coil Connector Overheating"
area: recalls
---

# Defect Description

Affected vehicles were built with ignition coil IC-2042-A whose electrical connector can overheat under sustained high-load operation, degrading the coil output. As the connection deteriorates, the engine may misfire and set DTC P0301 (cylinder 1 misfire detected) and, in cases affecting multiple cylinders, DTC P0300 (random/multiple cylinder misfire). A persistent misfire can lead to rough running, a stall risk, and potential catalyst damage. Diagnostic guidance for these codes is provided in the [Falcon Engine Service Manual :: Misfire Diagnosis](doc://man-falcon-engine#misfire-diagnosis) and the [Harrier Engine Service Manual :: Misfire Diagnostic Trouble Code Reference](doc://man-harrier-engine#diagnosis/misfire-diagnostic-trouble-code-reference). Owners noticing these symptoms should contact a dealer; recall details are posted at https://www.nhtsa.gov/recalls.

# Remedy

Dealers will replace ignition coil IC-2042-A with the redesigned coil IC-2042-B, which incorporates an upgraded connector and improved thermal margin. At the same time, spark plug SP-1108 will be inspected and replaced as required to ensure proper combustion after the coil swap. Coil identification and the IC-2042-A to IC-2042-B supersession are documented in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Coil Identification and Supersession](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils/coil-identification-and-supersession), with plug specifications in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Spark Plug Selection and Specifications](doc://man-heron-ignition#spark-plugs/spark-plug-selection-and-specifications). After installing IC-2042-B and SP-1108, clear any stored P0301 or P0300 and verify per the [Harrier Engine Service Manual :: Misfire Diagnosis Procedure](doc://man-harrier-engine#diagnosis/misfire-diagnosis-procedure). Further ignition reference is available in the [Heron Ignition Service Manual :: Ignition Coils](doc://man-heron-ignition#ignition-coils).
