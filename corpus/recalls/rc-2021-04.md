# Defect Description

On certain 2019-2020 Falcon vehicles with the 2.0T engine, the ignition coil
IC-2042-A connector can overheat under repeated heat cycles, which may lead to a
misfire (P0301) and, in rare cases, a thermal event.

# Remedy

Dealers will install the revised coil IC-2042-B at no charge. For the full
replacement procedure see
[Ignition Coil Replacement](doc://man-fal-3#engine/ignition-system/ignition-coil-replacement),
and for the superseding service guidance see [TSB-21-114](doc://tsb-21-114).
Owners can check recall status at [NHTSA](https://www.nhtsa.gov/recalls).
