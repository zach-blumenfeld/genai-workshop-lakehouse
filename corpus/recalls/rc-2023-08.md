title: Vega underhood fuse box corrosion can cause electrical failure and fire

# Defect Description

Certain Vega vehicles have an underhood fuse box, part number FUSE-9980, with an inadequate weather seal that allows moisture intrusion and terminal corrosion. The corrosion raises resistance and intermittently interrupts power and CAN distribution, which can set lost-communication codes U0100 and U0140 and, in severe cases, generate localized heating and fire. The fuse box and its harness interfaces are documented in the [Vega Electrical Service Manual :: Wiring Harness and Fuse Box](doc://man-vega-electrical#electrical-system-overview/wiring-harness-and-fuse-box), with code definitions under [Vega Electrical Service Manual :: Lost Communication with ECM/PCM](doc://man-vega-electrical#network-communication-diagnostic-codes/lost-communication-with-ecm-pcm) and [Vega Electrical Service Manual :: Lost Communication with BCM and Cluster](doc://man-vega-electrical#network-communication-diagnostic-codes/lost-communication-with-bcm-and-cluster).

# Safety Risk and Affected Vehicles

Corrosion-induced failure of FUSE-9980 can cause unexpected loss of electrical functions, stalling, and underhood fire, creating a crash and injury hazard. Affected vehicles are Vega units within the identified production period; review the [Vega Electrical Service Manual](doc://man-vega-electrical) and the [Vega Electrical Service Manual :: Electrical System Overview](doc://man-vega-electrical#electrical-system-overview) to confirm the electrical architecture before inspection.

# Remedy

Dealers will replace the underhood fuse box FUSE-9980 with a sealed-design service part at no charge and verify network integrity per procedure PROC-COMMS-DIAG. After replacement, clear U0100 and U0140 and confirm bus health using the [Vega Electrical Service Manual :: CAN Bus Communication Diagnosis Procedure](doc://man-vega-electrical#network-and-harness-sub-system-service/can-bus-communication-diagnosis-procedure) and the wiring references in [Vega Electrical Service Manual :: Wiring Diagrams and Reference](doc://man-vega-electrical#wiring-diagrams-and-reference). Owners can confirm recall applicability and remedy status at https://www.nhtsa.gov/recalls.
