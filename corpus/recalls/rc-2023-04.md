title: Marlin EV front brake hose may rupture reducing braking

# Defect Description

On affected Marlin EV vehicles, the front brake hose BH-7745 may have been manufactured with an out-of-spec inner liner that swells when exposed to brake fluid over time. As the liner degrades, the hose can balloon or rupture under pressure, releasing brake fluid BF-7790 and reducing hydraulic clamping force at the front calipers. Inspection and replacement should follow [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Hydraulic Lines and Fluid](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#hydraulic-lines-and-fluid). Technicians familiar with comparable platforms may also reference the [Vega Brakes Service Manual :: Front Brake Sub-System](doc://man-vega-brakes#front-brake-sub-system) for routing and torque context.

# Safety Risk and Affected Vehicles

A ruptured BH-7745 front brake hose causes a sudden loss of hydraulic pressure at the affected front wheel, increasing stopping distance and the risk of a crash. Affected vehicles are Marlin EV units built within the identified production window using brake hoses from the suspect supplier lot. Drivers may notice a soft or sinking brake pedal, a brake warning indicator, or visible fluid loss near the front wheel. Any vehicle exhibiting these symptoms should be removed from service until inspected per [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Brake Hose Replacement Procedure](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#hydraulic-lines-and-fluid/brake-hose-replacement-procedure).

# Remedy

Authorized dealers will replace both front brake hose BH-7745 assemblies with revised hoses and refill with fresh brake fluid BF-7790, free of charge. Replace the hoses following procedure PROC-BRAKE-HOSE as detailed in [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Brake Hose Replacement Procedure](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#hydraulic-lines-and-fluid/brake-hose-replacement-procedure), then perform procedure PROC-BRAKE-FLUID to flush and bleed the system per [Marlin EV Brakes Service Manual :: Brake Fluid Flush and Bleed Procedure](doc://man-marlin-ev-brakes#hydraulic-lines-and-fluid/brake-fluid-flush-and-bleed-procedure). Confirm a firm pedal and verify no fluid loss before returning the vehicle to the customer; the [Vega Brakes Service Manual :: Brake Fluid Flush and Bleed](doc://man-vega-brakes#hydraulic-sub-system/brake-fluid-flush-and-bleed) procedure offers a comparable bleed sequence for reference. Owners should contact their dealer to schedule this no-charge repair.
